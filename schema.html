PM- Kisan Yojana

Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi is an initiative by the government of India that give farmers up to â‚¹6,000 per year as minimum income support. The initiative was announced by Piyush Goyal during the 2019 Interim Union Budget of India on 1 February 2019.

What is the purpose of PM Kisan Samman Nidhi?

Under Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi Yojana, the government provides income support of Rs 6,000 per year to all farmer families who have cultivable land registered in their name, regardless of the size of the landholdings.

Who is eligible for PM Kisan Samman Nidhi Yojana?

Small and marginal farmers are eligible for PM-Kisan Yojana. Farmer families that own cultivable land can apply for the benefits of this yojana. The farmer should be an Indian citizen. In the Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi Yojana farmers from both rural and urban areas can register themselves.

What documents are required for PM Kisan Samman Nidhi?

  • Identity proof documents such as Aadhaar card, PAN card or driving licence.
  • Address proof documents like voter's ID card, ration card.
  • Bank detail documents like passbook and other account details.
  • Nominee details such as their Aadhaar card or PAN card, or income details

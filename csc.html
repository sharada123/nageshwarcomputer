Permanant Account Number(PAN CARD)

Permanent Account Number or PAN card is a crucial piece of document when it comes to taxation. It also serves as identity proof so that you can avail of a multitude of financial services, including opening a bank account, investing in Mutual Funds, or applying for a Credit Card.

Documents:

  • 2 Passport size photo
  • Adhar Card Xerox
  • Represenative Adhar Card (Minor case)

Aadhar Card

A 12-digit unique identity for every Indian individual, including children and infants. Enables identification for every resident Indian. Establishes uniqueness of every individual on the basis of demographic and biometric information.

Documents:

  • Person physically available
  • Birth Certificate
  • Name Proof (Name update)
  • Address Proof (Address update)
  • Date Of Birth Proof (DOB update)

Shop Act

Shop Act License is a type of legal permission which legally allows businesses or establishments to do business in a particular place. The registration process for a shop act licence is handled and coordinated through the Shop and Establishment Act in India, which although, has the same rules and result, varies from one Indian State to the other. Individuals, business owners and entrepreneurs with a physical shop or an office used to conduct business have to have their licences in place. These licences are proof of a particular business done at that place. However, as per the Act, all shops and commercial establishments operating within each state are covered by the respective Shop & Establishments Act.

Documents:

  • Passport Photo-1
  • Aadhar Card
  • Shop Photo

Food License

Fssai License India is a government-mandated license that is required for food businesses to operate in the country. It ensures that all food products manufactured, processed, stored, distributed or sold in India adhere to the standards and regulations set by the Food Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI).

Documents:

  • Photo-1
  • Aadhar card
  • Pan Card
  • Shop Act License
  • Grampanchayat Parvana

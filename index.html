Vision Of CSC & E-seva

The CSC scheme envisions the developement of common service centers as a trusted information technology-enabled network for connecting the citizens in every locality with the government,businesses and other formal sectors of the economy with deep impact on all three sectors of the indian economy.

Common service centers aim to provide the rural information access backed with end-to-end services and infrastructure to provide the rural population opportunities to enhance their quality of life.

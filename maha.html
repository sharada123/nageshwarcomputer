Cast Certificate

The Maharashtra caste certificate is an essential document to take advantage of scholarships or admission reserved for a particular caste in educational institutions. It is also necessary to submit the caste certificate to get government jobs or promotions in the reservation quota.

Documents:

  • Residential Self Declaration
  • Applicant's School Leaving Certificate/Bonafide
  • Ration Card Xerox
  • Adhar Card Xerox
  • 7/12 and 8a
  • applicant/father 2 passport size photo

Nationality And Domacile

A domicile represents an individual's permanent legal home, with the intent to return. Importantly, one may have multiple residences but only one domicile. Why do these terms matter? Your nationality and domicile status can potentially subject you to inheritance taxes in a country, even if you are not a resident there.

Documents:

  • Residential Self Declaration
  • Applicant's School Leaving Certificate/Bonafide
  • Ration Card Xerox
  • Adhar Card Xerox
  • Electricity Bill
  • applicant/father 2 passport size photo
